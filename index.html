Dynamic and chaotic

I like exploring darker themes in my work and much of my work is inspired by my love of the horror genre. I like to create dynamic and chaotic pieces that convey a strong sense of atmosphere. I mostly use acrylics, and more recently, I have been exploring mixed media.
